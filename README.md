# Velora

### An AI-Powered Mystery Dining Experience

> "You choose the boundaries. Velora chooses the surprise."

---

## Overview

Velora is a restaurant experience platform that transforms traditional food ordering into a personalized mystery.

Instead of selecting a specific dish from a menu, customers define their preferences, dietary restrictions, budget, and food boundaries. The system then identifies all eligible dishes and secretly selects one to be prepared and served.

The customer knows the meal will match their requirements, but they never know exactly what they are getting until it arrives at the table.

The goal is not to recommend the best dish. The goal is to create a safe, personalized, and exciting dining experience built around discovery and surprise.

---

## The Problem

Many customers spend a significant amount of time deciding what to order. Large menus often lead to decision fatigue, repetitive choices, and missed opportunities to explore new dishes.

Traditional restaurant systems focus on helping customers choose.

Velora focuses on helping customers discover.

---

## How It Works

### Step 1: Preference Collection

Customers fill out a preference form within the application.

The form may include:

* Vegetarian or Non-Vegetarian preference
* Preferred protein source (Chicken, Seafood, Red Meat, Eggs, etc.)
* Spice tolerance
* Budget range
* Allergies and dietary restrictions
* Ingredients they dislike
* Ingredients they refuse to eat

### Step 2: Eligibility Analysis

The system filters the restaurant's menu and creates a pool of dishes that satisfy all customer requirements.

### Step 3: Mystery Selection

A Mystery Selection Engine chooses one dish from the eligible pool.

The available dishes remain hidden from the customer.

### Step 4: Kitchen Processing

The selected dish is sent directly to the chef dashboard as a standard restaurant order.

### Step 5: The Reveal

The customer discovers the selected dish only when it is served.

### Safe Surprise

Selects dishes that closely match the customer's preferences.

### Explorer Mode

Encourages customers to try dishes they might not normally choose.

### Chef's Surprise

Provides the highest level of mystery while still respecting all dietary boundaries and restrictions.
